FOSPHOR

Finance + Phosphor. A local-first personal finance tracker with a retro CRT aesthetic. All data stays in your browser - nothing is sent online.

Portfolio Tracking

Track your assets across 8 categories: Savings, FD, PPF, Equity, Mutual Funds, Gold Bonds, PF, and Physical Assets. Use the tabs to switch between categories and add entries.

Snapshots

Click "Update" to save a snapshot of your current portfolio. This lets you compare your current values against the previous snapshot to see gains/losses.

Import / Export

Export your data as JSON to backup or transfer. Import a previously exported file to restore your data.

Utils → Style

Choose from 4 UI styles: Terminal (default), Modern, Minimal, or Retro (DOS aesthetic with IBM VGA font).

Utils → Notepad

A simple notepad that auto-saves to your browser. Supports inline calculations - type doMath(5 + 3) and it evaluates to 8.

Utils → Stock Average Pro

Calculate how buying more shares affects your average price. Enter your current holding, then add scenarios to compare different buy prices and quantities.

Theme Toggle

Click the ☀/☽ icon to switch between light and dark themes.
